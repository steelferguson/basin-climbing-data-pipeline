# Birthday Party Flow: Voice & Tone Comparison

## The Problem

The original flow messaging sounded like generic marketing copy - polished, emoji-heavy, and impersonal. It didn't match Basin's brand voice.

---

## Basin's Brand Voice (from Style Guide)

- **Down-to-earth** and humble
- **Inviting** - everyone feels they belong
- **Community-oriented** - connection over conversion
- Not corporate or salesy
- Like a friend, not a brand
